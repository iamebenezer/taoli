About

MEMECOIN X ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

TAOLIE began as a community-trusted memecoin on the Solana blockchain, originally aimed at providing a reliable core dev team that can be trusted. Evolving beyond its origins, TAOLIE now aims to offer a decentralized compute marketplace, enabling global access to affordable compute resources. Initially, we demonstrated our proof of concept by supplying compute resources through a network of private GPUs and system providers. Having successfully scaled and created significant value with our initial project on the Bittensor network, we are now poised for the next phase of our journey with TAOLIE.
